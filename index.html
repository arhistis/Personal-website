Who am I?

I am a student passioned about web-developing, coding, origami, pixel art, drawing, editing photos and many more. My name is Gabriel Cojea. I'm 20 years old, I live in Bucharest, Romania, and I study at The University of Mathematics and Computer Science. What is my dream? I wish to work as a "front-end developer", because I see websites as empty sheets of paper which let you build from nothing.
